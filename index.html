Privacy Policy for PSLE Ace Last updated: July 2026 PSLE Ace ("the App") is developed to help Primary 6 students in Singapore prepare for the PSLE Science examination. This policy explains what information the App collects and how it is used. Information We Collect Locally stored data: Your quiz answers, progress, scores, and app preferences are stored directly on your device using local storage. This data is not transmitted to us or any third party, and stays on your device unless you uninstall the app. Purchase information: If you unlock the full version of the App, purchase processing is handled by Google Play Billing and RevenueCat, our subscription management provider. RevenueCat may receive purchase-related data (such as transaction status and entitlement information) necessary to verify your purchase. We do not receive or store your payment card details — these are handled entirely by Google Play. No account creation: The App does not require you to create an account, and we do not collect your name, email address, or other personal identifiers to use the App. Children's Privacy The App is intended for use by students preparing for the PSLE examination, including children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. All progress data is stored locally on the device and is not linked to any personally identifiable information. Advertising The App does not currently display third-party advertisements. Data Sharing We do not sell or share your data with third parties, except as necessary to process purchases through Google Play Billing and RevenueCat as described above. Changes to This Policy We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
